Why From Anderson Exists

An MBA program is only as valuable as what you do with what you learn. This is what I am doing with it.

The Idea Behind the Series

I started the UCLA Anderson EMBA program in 2025 while working full-time in Digital Product Marketing at PENN Entertainment. The coursework is rigorous, the cases are excellent, and the frameworks are genuinely useful. But useful frameworks have a way of staying in notebooks and slide decks unless you do something intentional to bring them out.

From Anderson is that something intentional. Each installment takes one concept from the program, explains it in plain language, and connects it to the kinds of decisions that marketing and business leaders actually face. No academic jargon. No hedging. Just the idea, why it matters, and a question worth thinking about.

The goal is to make the investment in the program visible and useful to the people I work with, and to anyone else who finds their way here.

How Each Installment Is Structured
  • Part 1
    The Framework — where the idea comes from, what it actually says, and the case or context that makes it concrete.
  • Part 2
    The Application — how the framework connects to real marketing and business decisions. Forward-looking and practical, not theoretical.
  • Part 3
    The Question Worth Asking — one provocation to close. Not a prescribed answer. Just a question worth sitting with.
About Me

I work in Digital Product Marketing at PENN Entertainment, one of the largest gaming and entertainment companies in North America. My work sits at the intersection of digital product, CRM and loyalty operations, and brand and campaign execution. Most of what I do involves figuring out how to connect those things in a way that actually moves the business.

I enrolled in the UCLA Anderson EMBA program because I wanted a structured way to build the strategic and financial fluency that the job increasingly demands. From Anderson is part of how I am making that worthwhile beyond my own development.
